Composition of Sub Committee

(Extract from the Punjab Govt.Gaz, dated the 8th November, 2013)
GOVERNMENT OF PUNJAB
DEPARTMENT OF DEFENCE SERVICES WELFARE
(DEFENCE WELFARE BRANCH)

NOTIFICATION

The 31st October, 2013


No . 12/76/92-3DW/Part-II/7D/117452. In partial modification of Punjab Government Notification issued vide Endst. No. 12/76/92-3DW/Part-II/7DW/561 dated 26th April 2012, the Government of Punjab is pleased to re-constitute the Sub - Committee consisting of the following for the Management of Sainik Rest Houses which will look after the repair/maintenance work of all Sainik Rest Houses in the State and other matters entrusted to it :-

  1. (a) Chairman - Principal Secretary/Secretary, Defence Services Welfare
  2. (b) Members - Three ESM (To be nominated by Chairman)
  3. (c) In attendance - Officer on Special Duty (OSD)
  4. (d) Member Secretary - Director, Sainik Welfare, Punjab Chandigarh.

S.R. Ladhar
Chandigarh    Secretary to Government of Punjab
The,   14th October, 2013   Defence Services Welfare Department

0352/11-2013/Pb. Govt. Press, S.A.S Nagar
