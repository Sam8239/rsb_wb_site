Training Conducted By the Department

TRAINING

1. Pre-Recruitment/Sainik Vocational Training Centres(SVTC)/Sainik Institute of Management & Technology(SIMT)  are being run at various District Headquarters on the directions of Govt of Punjab through Directorate of Sainik Welfare Punjab. This is a welfare scheme authorized under DSW .2 Plan Scheme to impart vocational and Recruitment training to the Ex-servicemen, their widows and wards to enhance chances of their employment and re-settlement in the civil. The wards of SC/ST, Para Military Forces and other weaker sections of society are also covered under this scheme. List of Sainik Vocational Training Centres is as under:-

  1. a)         Sainik Institute of Management & Technology, Amritsar
  2. b)         Sainik Institute of Management & Technology, Bathinda

  5. e)         Sainik Vocational Training Centre, Fatehgarh Sahib

2. Computer Training

All Sainik Institutes of Management & Technology/Sainik Vocational Training Centers of this department imparting computer training. Sainik Institute of Management & Technology,  Amritsar, Bathinda, Faridkot, Gurdaspur, Hoshiarpur, Jalandhar, Sri Mukatsar Sahib, Roopnagar & SAS Nagar  are  approved for Regular College under Punjab Technical University , Jalandhar and are  running computer courses PGDCA, B.Sc(IT) & M.Sc.(IT). Academic session starts form Jul-Aug month for these courses. 
Criteria for selection of Students and their Strength :- Under normal circumstances, 20 students will be enrolled for one academic session per stream. In addition, up to 20% extra admission will be done to cater for the drop outs at a later stage. The minimum qualification will be as per the norms of the Punjab Technical University. Higher qualified candidates will be given preference. The candidates seeking admission will undergo screening test/interview by the Selection Committee. A detailed prospectus giving out all the details is being published shortly.

3. Pre Recruitment Training

The responsibility of these centers is to train, prepare & motivate  wards of Ex-servicemen and the youth of Punjab to join the Armed Forces and other uniformed services.  This training (Pre-recruitment Training) is being run with the sanctioned funds from the Punjab Govt. at Amritsar, Fatehgarh Sahib, Gurdaspur, Hoshiarpur, Jalandhar, Kapurthala, Ludhiana, Patiala, Roopnagar & Sangrur.  Recruitment schedule for the complete year is issued by DGR and Army Recruitment Zones, Amritsar, Jalandhar, Ludhiana and Patiala. Accordingly training is conducted in District Defence Services Welfare Offices for  two months prior to the recruitment rally. The current schedule is as under :-
Criteria for selection of Students and their Strength :- The selection process of students will remain the same as mentioned above for Computer & Stenography courses. However, they should be declared medically fit after proper medical examination by a medical officer as per laid out standards by the Recruiting Directorate before enrolling into the training.


4. Stenography & Typing Training

Sainik Institute of Management & Technology, Amritsar & Roopnagar  are imparting Stenography and Typing Training and are affiliated with the Punjab Language Department and the syllabus is planned by them and centrally adopted by all stenography Institutions in Punjab.  The Minimum qualification for entry is Graduation,  however, higher qualified candidates will be preferred. The aspirant candidates will be put through screening test/interview. The Selection Committee after conducting the test/interview will put up the board proceedings and the list of approved candidates to the Principal ( DSWO) for final selection. The strength has been laid out by the Language Department as 20, however, this can be can  increased  20% to cater for drop outs at a later stage.   


5. Pre-Commission Training

The responsibility of these centers is to train the wards of Ex-servicemen and youth of Punjab to join the Armed Forces and other uniformed services as commissioned officers. This training is called Pre Commission Training, (PCT) which is being run with the sanctioned funds from Punjab  Govt.  Presently, this training is conducted at Amritsar, Bathinda, Jalandhar and Patiala.  The aim is to ensure sustained and quality intake of officers from Punjab into the Defence Service and other uniformed services.

6.  Short Term Computer Courses (Certified by ISO 9001:2008)

The Department  is also  running short term computer courses  certified by ISO 9001:2008 in  Sainik Institutes of Management & Technology/Sainik Vocational Training Centres . Ex-servicemen/Serving soldiers and their dependents can register their names for admission . The detail of Courses is as under:-
a)         Basic Computer Course (3 months)
b)         Programming in C (3 months)
c)         Programming in C++ (3 months)
d)         HTML & Web Designing (4 months)
e)         Programming in Java Core + Adv. (3 months)
