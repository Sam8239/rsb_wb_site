Old Age Pension

Amount

  • Rs. 300/- per month upto 28/05/2006
  • Rs.500/- per month w.e.f 29/5/2006
  • Rs.1000/- per month w.e.f 29/01/2008
  • Rs.2000/- per month w.e.f 14/02/2013
  • Rs.4500/- per month w.e.f 27/12/2016

Eligibility

  • Ex-Servicemen/Widows of Ex-Servicemen of World War-II above the age of 65 years who are not in receipt of any pension.

Documents Required

  • Application on specific proforma attached at Annexure I
  • Age assessed by Medical Officer
  • A certificate from Distt Social & Security Officer regarding non pensioner attached as per Annexure II.
  • Affidavit as per Annexure III
  • Discharge certificate
  • Photostat attested copy of Ration Card.
